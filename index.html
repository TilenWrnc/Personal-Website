About Me

My name is Tilen Bratina, and I come from a small but beautiful country Slovenia. While I’ve always been fascinated by computers and technology, I initially chose a different path and pursued a career in nursing. I completed both nursing high school and college, but during my studies, I realized that this field wasn’t the right fit for me.

That realization led me to explore my long-standing interest in programming. I began my journey with the CS50 course by Harvard University on YouTube, which gave me a strong foundation. Soon after, I discovered The Odin Project, an excellent online curriculum that guided me through the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Git.

My goal is to become a successful full-stack developer, with a particular interest in backend technologies. I'm passionate about building functional, user-friendly applications and continuously learning new tools and frameworks to grow as a developer.
